Rolls in Call of Cthulhu

Most rolls of Call of Cthulhu are a 'check roll' performed with a D100:
- Fumble: Roll 100/if skill bellow 50, roll 96 or more;
- Failure: Any number above the skill;
- Success: Equal to or bellow skill level;
- Hard Success: Half the skill or lower;
- Extreme Success: 1/5 the skill or lower;
- Critical Success: Roll of 01;

In skill rolls, the Keeper can decide difficulty.
In opposed rolls, levels of success are compared and penalty/bonus dies may be awarded;
